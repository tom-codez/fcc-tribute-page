Elon Musk

A man who is pushing world evolution forward, one company at a time.

Here's a time line of Elon's life, so far:

  • 1971: Born in Pretoria, South Africa.
  • 1981: Interest in Computing.
  • 1983: Sold his first video game, Blastar.
  • 1992: Went to University of Pennsylvania to Study Business and Physics.
  • 1995: Started Zip2, a Web Software Company.
  • 1999: Sold Zip2 to Compaq for US$307 million.
  • 1999: Cofounded X.com, a financial service and payment company.
  • 2000: X.com merged with Confinity to form PayPal, Musk married his first wife.
  • 2002: Founded SpaceX, a space exploration company.
  • 2004: Musk's wife gave birth to twin sons.
  • 2004: Musk joined Tesla.
  • 2006: Musk's wife gave birth to triplets, Cofounded SolarCity and SpaceX Success.
  • 2007: Musk became the CEO of Tesla.
  • 2012: Announced SolarCity and Tesla Collaboration.
  • 2013: SolarCity became the second largest solar power provider.
  • 2015: Announced; Hyperloop design competition.
  • 2015: Announced creation on OpenAi, Artificial Intelligence company.
  • 2016: Musk co-founded Neuralink and The Boring Company.
  • 2017: Hyperloop One announced first successful test run.
  • 2017: Hyperloop One announced first successful test run. Tesla opens its first gigafactory.
  • 2021: Overtakes Jeff Bezos, to become the richest man in the world.
  • 2022: Musk offers to buy Twitter.
